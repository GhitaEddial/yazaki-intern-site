How to Connect with Professionals

Read Name Badges & Connect on LinkedIn

As an intern at Yazaki Morocco, you can easily connect with any professional you're interested in learning from. Simply:

1

Look for their name badge at their desk or workstation

2

Note their full name and position from the badge

3

Search for them on LinkedIn using their name and "Yazaki Morocco"

4

Send a professional connection request mentioning your internship

Professional Networking Guidelines

Professional Approach

Always maintain a professional tone when reaching out. Introduce yourself clearly and mention your internship program at Yazaki Morocco.

Be Specific

Clearly state your purpose for connecting. Whether it's career advice, project guidance, or industry insights - be direct and respectful.

Respect Time

Be mindful of professionals' busy schedules. Keep initial messages concise and suggest flexible meeting times that work for them.

Show Gratitude

Always thank professionals for their time and insights. Follow up with updates on how their advice helped your development.

Networking Success Tips

01

Craft a Compelling Message

Start with a personalized greeting, mention your internship at Yazaki Morocco, and clearly state your purpose for connecting. Keep it concise but engaging.

02

Research Before Reaching Out

Observe their work area and note their department. Reference specific projects or achievements to show genuine interest in their work.

03

Follow Up Professionally

Send a thank you message after any interaction. Share updates on how their advice helped you grow professionally.

04

Offer Value in Return

Share relevant articles, insights from your studies, or offer to help with projects where your skills might be useful.
